Imagine..


Imagine if you were being on your death bed

and standing around your bed

Are the ghosts of your ideas,

your dreams

your abilities...


all GIVEN to you and you ONLY by life

and you for whatever reason

with the time you were given

NEVER ACTED on those ideas

never PERSUED that dream

the world that you lived in never saw your leadership

for we never heard your voice


so there they are standing around you...

LOOKING AT YOU

with large, angry eyes saying...

WE CAME TO YOU

FOR ONLY YOU COULD HAVE GIVEN US LIFE

but now, we must DIE with you forever...


the question is...

if you died today...

what ideas...

what talents...

what dreams...

Will DIE with you?
